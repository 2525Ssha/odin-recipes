Tuna Bean Sprouts

You can make this in 5 minutes!

Ingredients

  • 200g bean sprouts
  • 70g canned tuna
  • 2 tbsp Metsuyu soup base>
  • 1tbsp sesame oil
  • 1tbsp sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Wash and place the bean sprouts in a microwavable bowl.
  2. Add drained canned tuna and Japanese Mentsuyu soup base.
  3. Cover with plastic wrap then microwave on medium (600W) for 3 minutes.
  4. Toss with sesame oil and sesame seeds to finish.
