Brussels sprouts Namul

You can make this in 5 minutes too!

Ingredients

  • 200g brussels sprouts
  • 1/4tsp salt
  • 1tbsp sesame oil
  • 1tsp toasted white sesame seeds

Directions

  1. Wash brussels sprouts, trim off the dirty end, then slice down the middle lengthwise.
  2. Boil water in a large pot. Add a teaspoon of salt in the water, cook brussels sprouts for about 3 minutes.
  3. Drain well in a strainer.
  4. Toss with salt, sesame oil, and sesame seeds to finish.
