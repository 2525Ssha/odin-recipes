Tuna Mayo Cucumber

When you want something easy and delicious, this is the meal for you

Ingredients

  • 1 Japanese cucumber
  • 4 tbsp. well drained canned tuna
  • 1 tbsp. mayonnaise
  • 1/3 tsp. salt
  • pepper

Directions

  1. 1. Slice the cucumber into thin rounds (you can use a vegetable slicer if you have).
  2. Sprinkle with a bit of salt. When they become limp, drain well and gently squeeze with a paper towel.
  3. *Removing excess water from the cucumber will keep the salad from becoming wet.
  4. Combine cucumber slices, tuna, mayo and pepper in one bowl to finish.
